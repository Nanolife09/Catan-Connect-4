Game Screen

This Is What You Will See When The Game Starts.

Both Sides Of The Screen Shows Resources And Development Card That Each Player Have.
You Can Use Your Development Card By Clicking The Development Card In Your Inventory.

The Center Of The Screen Is The Board.
Board Will Be Different For Each Game
When You Select A Column Of The Board, The Mark Will Fill The Board From Bottom To Top

X

Game Objective

You Can Win By Connecting 4 Marks Vertically, Horizontally, Or Diagnally

You Can Also Win By Buying 10 Victory Points

When The Board Is Completely Filled With Marks, The Player With Most Victory Points Wins

X

How to play

You Can Buy 2 Development Card
OR
Use 1 Development Card

You Can Trade 2 Identical Resources To 1 Any Resource Maximum of 3 Times Per Turn

Place Your Mark At The End Of Your Turn To Obtain One Resource Where Your Mark Lands
